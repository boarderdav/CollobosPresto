Enterprise AirPrint

Presto is a robust solution for enterprise mobile printing. Presto integrates with a corporate network’s DNS servers leveraging one-of-a-kind cross platform technology for client devices and print hardware manufacturers. Presto leverages the same technology across iOS, OS X, Android, and Chrome OS. Presto is the premier solution for mobile print with Magic Queue (pull printing), QR Code Scan release, Printer Geolocation, Print Management Integration and more. Supported Client Operating Systems: iOS 10.7+, Android 5.0+, Chrome 44+
